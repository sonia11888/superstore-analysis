#  Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to understand profitability, sales trends, and category performance.

##  Dataset
Sample Superstore Dataset (Kaggle)

## Tools Used
- R
- ggplot2
- dplyr

## Key Insights

- Technology produces the highest profit and great sales performance.
- Office Supplies is the highly profitable category in terms of profit margin.
- Furniture has high sales but very low profit, showing pricing or discount issues.

##  Visualizations

- Profit by Category
- Sales vs Profit Relationship

##  Business Recommendation

Concentrate on enhancing Furniture profitability while expanding Technology sales strategies.
